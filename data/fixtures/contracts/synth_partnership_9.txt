Limited Partnership Agreement among Nu Capital Partners, Xi Holdings, and Omicron Investments, executed July 20, 2026. This Agreement is to be construed in accordance with and governed by the laws of the State of New Jersey. Any limited partner may withdraw from the partnership at its convenience upon one hundred eighty (180) days advance written notice to the General Partner. The General Partner's aggregate cumulative liability to the limited partners shall not exceed three million U.S. dollars (\$3,000,000). The interests of the limited partners may not be assigned, pledged, or transferred without the General Partner's prior written approval.
